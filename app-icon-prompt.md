# FitMunch App Icon Prompt

## DALL-E PROMPT FOR 1024x1024 ICON

**Prompt:**
A minimalist, bold, vibrant icon featuring a stylized fork and knife forming a checkmark shape, with a fresh green leaf sprouting from the handle. Clean white background, flat design with subtle gradient shading. The icon should use bright, appetizing colors - primarily fresh green (#4CAF50) and warm orange (#FF9800) with clean white accents. The design should be simple, recognizable at small sizes, and work on both light and dark backgrounds.

**Alternative prompts if needed:**
1. "A clean, modern app icon of a fork and spoon forming a heart shape with a small leaf accent, vibrant green and orange colors, minimalist flat design, white background"
2. "Stylized plate with fork and knife crossed over it, with a small leaf growing from the plate, bright colors, clean lines, app icon style"
3. "Minimalist nutrition tracking app icon featuring a fork with measurement markings like a ruler, fresh green color, clean white background"

## REQUIREMENTS CHECKLIST
- ✅ Single focal element (fork/knife/leaf combination)
- ✅ No text or letters
- ✅ Bold, vibrant colors (primary: fresh green)
- ✅ Readable at 60x60px (test by scaling down)
- ✅ Works on both light and dark backgrounds
- ✅ Clean, modern, minimalist design
- ✅ Appetizing and health-focused aesthetic
- ✅ Square format with proper padding

## COLOR PALETTE
- Primary: Fresh Green (#4CAF50)
- Secondary: Warm Orange (#FF9800) 
- Accent: Clean White (#FFFFFF)
- Optional: Dark Green (#2E7D32) for shading

## DESIGN NOTES
- The fork and knife should form a recognizable shape (checkmark, heart, or crossed)
- Include a leaf element to emphasize health/nutrition
- Use flat design with subtle gradients for depth
- Ensure good contrast for accessibility
- Avoid complex details that won't scale down
- Make it feel modern, clean, and trustworthy
