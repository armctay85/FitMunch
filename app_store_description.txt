
# FitMunch - Your Complete Fitness & Nutrition Companion

## App Store Short Description (170 characters)
Plan healthy meals, track workouts, and achieve your fitness goals with FitMunch - the all-in-one app for your health and fitness journey.

## App Store Full Description
Transform your fitness journey with FitMunch, the ultimate health and fitness companion app that combines meal planning, workout tracking, and progress monitoring in one seamless experience.

### Plan Your Meals with Precision
- Create personalized meal plans based on your dietary preferences and goals
- Access our extensive database of healthy, delicious recipes 
- Generate shopping lists automatically from your meal plans
- Track macros and calories with our intuitive nutrition counter

### Optimize Your Workouts
- Choose from hundreds of pre-designed workout routines or create your own
- Follow step-by-step exercise instructions with HD video demonstrations
- Track your progress with detailed workout logs and performance metrics
- Receive personalized workout recommendations based on your goals

### Monitor Your Progress
- Track key metrics like weight, body measurements, and exercise performance
- Visualize your progress with intuitive charts and analytics
- Set achievable goals and celebrate milestones
- Maintain motivation with progress insights and achievements

### Features for Everyone
- Beginners: Guided programs to help you start your fitness journey
- Intermediate: Varied workouts and meal plans to keep you challenged
- Advanced: Custom programming and detailed analytics for optimizing performance
- Nutrition enthusiasts: Recipe creation, meal planning, and precise macro tracking

### Sync Across All Your Devices
Access your FitMunch data seamlessly across your iPhone, iPad, and Apple Watch. Your fitness journey stays with you, no matter which device you're using.

Take control of your fitness journey today with FitMunch - where nutrition meets exercise for a healthier you.
