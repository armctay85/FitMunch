### Section 9: Data Sharing
- Does the app share user data with third parties? **No**
- Does the app use data for advertising purposes? **No**

## Summary
The FitMunch app is suitable for all ages (Everyone) as it does not contain any violent, sexual, profane, or controlled substance content. It does collect basic personal information for fitness tracking purposes, but this data is stored securely and not shared with third parties. The app offers in-app purchases through Google Play's billing system for premium subscription features.
