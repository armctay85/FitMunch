Getting Started

FitMunch helps you track nutrition, scan receipts, plan meals, and stay on top of your food goals. New to FitMunch? Start with our homepage or check pricing.

Common Questions

How does receipt scanning work?

Upload a photo of your supermarket receipt. FitMunch extracts items, matches nutrition data, and adds everything to your pantry and meal plan.

Can my personal trainer see my data?

Only if you connect your account to their PT dashboard via an invite link. You control sharing at all times.

How do I cancel?

Email support@fitmunch.com.au with your account email. We'll process cancellations within 2 business days.
