Refund Policy

Subscription refunds

FitMunch subscriptions are billed monthly via Stripe. You can cancel at any time — your access continues until the end of the current billing period.

14-day refund window

If you're not satisfied within your first 14 days, email support@fitmunch.com.au for a full refund. After 14 days, refunds are at our discretion on a case-by-case basis.

How to request a refund

Email support@fitmunch.com.au with:

  • Your account email address
  • The reason for your refund request

We'll respond within 1–2 business days.

Processing

Approved refunds are processed via Stripe and typically appear in your account within 5–10 business days, depending on your bank.

General food planning support only. Not medical advice.
