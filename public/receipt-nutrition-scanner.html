Why Use a Receipt Scanner for Nutrition?

Manual food logging is tedious. Most people give up after three days. A receipt scanner eliminates data entry — just snap and go. It catches everything you bought, not just what you remembered to log. And it surfaces patterns you'd miss: "Your Saturday shops are 40% higher in sugar" or "You buy 3x more snacks when you shop hungry."

FitMunch is built in Australia, for Australian products. Our food database covers Coles, Woolworths, and Aldi house brands — not US-centric barcode databases that miss half your shop.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the receipt scanner free?
Your first receipt scan is free. Unlimited scanning is included in FitMunch plans starting at $59/month for trainers and coming soon for consumers.
Does it work with crumpled or faded receipts?
Yes. Our AI handles poor lighting, wrinkles, and faded thermal paper. If it can't read something, it'll flag the item for manual confirmation.
Do you store my receipts?
Receipt images are processed and discarded. We store the structured nutrition data to power your trends and meal plans. You can delete your data anytime.
How accurate is the nutrition data?
We use Australian food composition databases matched to supermarket product catalogues. Accuracy is typically within 5–10% for macro estimates. This is a food planning tool, not a medical device.
Can my personal trainer see my receipts?
Only if you connect with them through FitMunch for PTs and explicitly share your nutrition data. Your data is private by default.
