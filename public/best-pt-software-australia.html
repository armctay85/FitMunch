What to Look For When Choosing PT Software in Australia

1. AUD pricing

USD-only pricing means your monthly cost varies with the exchange rate. Over a year, this can add hundreds to your software bill. Look for platforms that bill in AUD.

2. Server location

Servers in the US mean noticeable lag when your Australian clients are logging workouts or checking meal plans during peak hours. AU or AP region servers make a real difference in day-to-day experience.

3. Client limits

Many platforms charge more per additional client. As your business grows, per-client pricing can quickly become your biggest overhead. Unlimited-client plans offer much better value at scale.

4. Nutrition + workout in one place

Most PTs use separate apps for workout programming and nutrition guidance. A combined platform means fewer tools to manage and a better experience for clients.

5. GST-ready invoicing

If you're registered for GST, you need software that generates compliant tax invoices. US-based software typically doesn't handle Australian tax requirements.
